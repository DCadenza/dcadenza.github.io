HOW IT WORKS

REGISTER

Download the app. Create a profile. Link to your bank account or debt/payroll card. If you don’t have a smartphone, no problem. You can sign up online.

EARN

After you clock out of your shift, your employer will verify the wages you earned. Once this happens, you will receive a notification through the app that you have funds available to cash out.

CASH OUT

Nowstapay is completely optional. If you’d like to get paid, simply click the “Cash Out” button in the app. If not, no problem. Nowstapay works on your schedule, which means you can pick and choose which shifts to cash out, and when, based on your finances.
