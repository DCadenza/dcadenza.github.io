COSMOS CERTIFIED LINE OF COSMETICS
Thu, 01/10/2019 - 17:43

EXSENS history began in 2014, when the Néo Cosmétique team created a unique collection of sensual cosmetics with recognizable and eye-catching kaleidoscope packaging. Exsens conveys enormous positive energy through its products. EXEKO products are ORGANIC CERTIFIED according to the COSMOS standard, what does it mean?

The COSMOS-standard AISBL is a not-for-profit, international association registered in Belgium. It was set up to own, manage and develop the COSMOS-standard as an international (and internationally accepted) standard for organic and natural cosmetics. The AISBL was founded in 2010 by the five main European (and international) organisations involved with organic and natural cosmetics standards: BDIH in Germany, Cosmebio and Ecocert in France, ICEA in Italy, and Soil Association in the UK.

Overall, COSMOS-certified products are produced to the highest standards for organic and natural cosmetics, and are safe, effective and good to use. They are easy to identify by their labels which contain all the information you need to choose a product that fulfils your needs and expectations* (* COSMOS - standard Cosmetics Organic and Natural Standard, Version 3.0 - including editorial changes, published 1 January 2019).
