SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT

ETHIC

Because EXSENS values its' environmental and social responsibility, the company pursues a global and complete environmental and social responsibility policy in different ways. Along with many other partners, we are promoting the use of developmentally disadvantaged workers through partnership with ESAT (Etablissement et Service d’Aide par le Travail).

TRANSPORT

Our transportation preferences are driven by our desire to reduce our daily impact on the environment. Our transporters are certified according to ISO 14001. We give priority to local suppliers. We buy raw materials primarily in France, otherwise in Europe, and seldom elswere

PACKAGING AND POS DISPLAYS

We limit our consumption of cardboard and other wasteful materials by reducing the size of our cases and displays as much as possible.

WASTE AND ENERGY MANAGEMENT

Our policy for waste management is dedicated to continuous efforts at recycling and waste reduction. We have practiced the systematic recycling of most of our waste since 2012. Our industrial waste disposal is managed by an ISO14001 certified partner.
