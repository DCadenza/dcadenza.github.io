HISTORY & FOOTPRINT
EXSENS history began in 2014, when the Néo Cosmétique team created a unique collection of sensual cosmetics with recognizable and eye-catching kaleidoscope packaging. Exsens conveys enormous positive energy through its products.
Sharp formulation expertise
With a dedicated Research and Development team, we work every day to create an exceptional end-to-end sensual experience with EXSENS. Our products are formulated with natural ingredients and sourced partly from organic farms (grown without synthetic fertilizers or pesticides). no GMOs no paraffin, silicone or PEG, no aluminium salts no synthetic preservatives not authorized under the Ecocert standard, such as parabens, phenoxyethanol, EDTA, and DMDM Hydantoin
High quality ingredients
a manufacturer with profound knowledge of the cosmetic market, we know that the quality of cosmetic products is significantly determined by the quality and goodness of the raw materials used. This means that all raw materials we use for the manufacturing of EXSENS products conform with defined specifications to guarantee a stable level of quality.
